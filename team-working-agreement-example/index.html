Team Norms

How we work together day-to-day

100% Honest & 100% Kind

We commit to direct, transparent communication delivered with empathy and respect

Prioritize meeting agendas

Every meeting has a clear purpose and expected outcomes

Share thoughts

Everyone is encouraged and expected to contribute their perspectives

Use tools situationally

Choose the right communication channel and tool for the context

Maintain high level of camaraderie

Build strong relationships and support each other beyond just work tasks

Camera is on for a meeting of less than 10 people

Foster connection and engagement in smaller group settings

Core working hours are 00:00a - 00:00p

Times when we expect to overlap and be available for synchronous collaboration
