Engineering Leadership

Becoming an Engineering Manager is a significant career inflection point. Rather than being the expert in a particular language or framework, you shift focus to achieving goals by building and leading a healthy team.

Over 13 sessions, we'll tackle key skills necessary as an Engineering Manager — from developing allies and investing in your team, to capacity planning and building a strong culture.

"Leadership is a journey. There is always time for more books."
