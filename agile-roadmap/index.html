Planning Notes

  • • Story Points: Use Fibonacci sequence (1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21). Points > 13 should be broken down.
  • • Sprint Length: Assume 2-week sprints. Adjust timelines accordingly.
  • • Dependencies: List other initiatives that must complete first. Watch for blockers.
  • • Risk Management: High-risk items need extra buffer time or contingency planning.
  • • Variance Tracking: Update Actual Days as work completes to improve future estimates.
