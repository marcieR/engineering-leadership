Real vs Ideal Analysis

Compare your current state with your desired state to identify gaps and create actionable improvement plans.

How to Use This Template

  1. 1 Real (Current State): Describe how things actually are today - be honest and specific
  2. 2 Ideal (Desired State): Describe how you want things to be - be aspirational but realistic
  3. 3 Gap Analysis: Identify the differences and what needs to change
  4. 4 Action Plan: Define concrete steps to bridge the gap
Category / Area Real (Current State) Ideal (Desired State)
Example: Team Communication

Current situation:

  • • Ad-hoc Slack messages with no clear channels
  • • Important updates get lost in threads
  • • No regular team sync meetings

Desired outcome:

  • • Organized Slack channels by project/topic
  • • Daily standup for critical updates
  • • Weekly team sync with clear agendas

Action Planning

Best Practices

  • • Be honest and specific when describing the current state - avoid sugar-coating
  • • Make ideal states aspirational but achievable - stretch goals, not fantasy
  • • Focus on 3-5 key areas rather than trying to fix everything at once
  • • Review and update this document quarterly to track progress
  • • Involve the whole team in creating and reviewing this analysis
